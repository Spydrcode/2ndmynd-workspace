1-5 Rubric

- Decision clarity: How clearly are next steps proposed? (1 = vague, 5 = crystal clear, prioritized)
- Signal fidelity: Are conclusions grounded in snapshot aggregates with no invented facts? (1 = many inventions, 5 = fully grounded)
- Actionability/prioritization: Are next steps limited, pragmatic, and ordered by effort/impact? (1 = speculative, 5 = precise and prioritized)
- Quiet Founder tone: Does the output avoid hype and sound like a focused founder? (1 = hype/verbose, 5 = calm, concise)
- Doctrine compliance: Does the output avoid dashboard/KPI/analytics/monitoring/BI language? (1 = repeated forbidden terms, 5 = clean)

Scoring guidance: Provide a numeric score and one-line justification for each category.
